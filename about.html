I'm a junior in Mechanical Engineering with an Aerospace Engineering minor at Kennesaw State University, graduating May 2027. I split my time between hands-on hardware — propulsion, airframes, ground support equipment — and CFD research validated against wind-tunnel and experimental data.

I currently serve as Vice President of the Kennesaw Rocketry Team, where I previously led Liquid Propulsion, and I spent this past summer as a Mechanical Design Engineer Intern at Textron GSE working on the electrification of airport ground support equipment. Before that, I led a 20-person team through the full design-manufacturing-flight-test cycle of an autonomous delivery aircraft as Team Lead of the Aerial Robotics Competition Team.

On the research side, I work in KSU's Advanced Aerodynamics and Propulsion Lab, where my CFD work on compound delta wings and blended wing body aircraft has led to a first-authored conference paper and co-authored and first-authored work accepted to AIAA SciTech.
