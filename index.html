Andrew Marion

I'm a junior in Mechanical Engineering, minoring in Aerospace, at Kennesaw State University, graduating May 2027. My work splits between hands-on hardware — rocket propulsion, autonomous aircraft, and ground support equipment — and CFD research validated against wind-tunnel and experimental data. I currently serve as Vice President of the Kennesaw Rocketry Team and spent this past summer as a Mechanical Design Engineer Intern at Textron GSE.
